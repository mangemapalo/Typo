Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with us

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2-4 business hours during working days (Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 6 PM). For weekend inquiries, we'll respond on the next business day. Urgent inquiries can be addressed via phone call at +260 960 355 692.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer a free 30-minute initial consultation for all new clients. During this consultation, we'll discuss your project requirements, goals, timeline, and budget. This helps us understand your needs and provide accurate recommendations and quotes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers (Zambian Kwacha), mobile money (Airtel Money, MTN Money, Zamtel Kwacha), and cash payments. For larger projects, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon project completion.

Do you work with clients outside Zambia?

Absolutely! While we're based in Zambia, we regularly work with clients across Africa and internationally. We're comfortable working across time zones and can conduct all meetings and communications remotely. We accept international payments through bank transfers.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, e-commerce stores 4-8 weeks, and custom applications 8-16 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation and project scoping.
